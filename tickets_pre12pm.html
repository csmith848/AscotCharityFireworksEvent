IMPORTANT INFORMATION - PLEASE READ CAREFULLY


Before you purchase your online tickets, please read all of the information here. This information is to ensure you understand the process and what you must bring with you when you arrive at the event.

Failure to follow this information may lead to you being refused entry to the event.

When you select to purchase tickets online (below) you will be transferred to the Ascot online ticket purchase portal. Within this portal you can select the number of Adult and Child tickets required. Remember children under 5 do not require a ticket. Child tickets are for children between 5 & 12 years of age inclusive. Children 13 years or over require an Adult ticket.
All children must be accompanied by an adult, including those with an adult ticket.

After completion of your purchase you will print each ticket out.
If you cannot print tickets now, please do not proceed.
You must bring all your printed tickets with you to the gate to be scanned and allow entry.
Please ignore any messages on the portal about collecting tickets or postal delivery - this is not possible.
Please note it is not possible to have replacement tickets issued or to order them by telephone.
Please also ignore any messages about parking passes. These are not required as free parking is available - see the directions section on the details page for further information.

There may be a fee charged for using this service - this will be confirmed before you commit to buy.
Tickets will be available until midday on the day of the event.
After this time please purchase tickets at the gate.

Spectacular Fireworks set to music
Ascot Racecourse Grandstand, Ascot
Gates open 4:00pm, Fireworks start 6:00pm

Prices at gate:  Adults £9.00, Children £7.00, Under 5s free
Prices in advance:  Adults £6.50, Children £4.50, Under 5s free

Adult tickets for age 13+, Children 5-12 years old

Under 18s must be accompanied by an adult

Bypass the queues and make use of fast track entrance to the event by buying tickets in advance here.

Information

Ascot Charity Fireworks Event is organised by Ascot Round Table.
Only tickets purchased from Ascot Round Table or through one of its resellers raise money for charity.
These tickets are available from local shops and schools as listed on the home page.
Tickets will be available via our online ticket portal from early October or alternatively at the gate on the night.
Race day ticket holders my stay and watch the event, but these tickets do not contribute to charity.

Free car parking in Car Park 5A.
Disabled parking available for disabled badge holders.
Check local signs and directions provided on the night as additional car parks may be utilised if busy.

This year the fireworks viewing will take place from the Grandstand of Ascot Racecourse.
This fabulous location will provide the best view of the largest show ever to be held by the organisers.
In addition there will be a fun fair. Food, drinks and licensed bars are also available.

Ascot Round Table is a local organisation with International links and raises funds for local charities through this Fireworks event, the Christmas Santa Sleigh, Golf Tournament and other fundraising activities.

Charities and organisations that have benefited from the event in the past include:

  • SportsAble
  • Chandler Day Centre, Sunninghill
  • Thames Valley Air Ambulance
  • Thames Hospicecare
  • Charters Youth & Community Centre
  • Youth Speaks
  • Young Chef
  • Macmillian

We also support a number of smaller applications and individual cases.
If you'd like us to consider an application please use the contact us page.

Directions

By Car

Free parking will be available in car park 5A. Access to the car park is off Ascot High street. Additional car parks may be utilised if busy so please use these directions as a guide to the location, and follow the directions of the car park marshals on the night.

From the North (Windsor/Winkfield/Maidenhead), approach Ascot on the A332 (Windsor Road). At the double mini-roundabout, turn left and proceed along Winkfield Road. At next roundabout turn right towards Ascot High Street. Car park 5A is on the left, before you are opposite the Jaguar garage.

From the West(Bracknell), approach Ascot on the A329. Go straight ahead at the Heatherwood Hospital roundabout and proceed down the high street, past the shops. Just after the Jaguar garage of the left, car park 5A is on the right hand side.

From the East (Sunningdale), approach Ascot on the A329. Go over mini-roundabout into the High Street. Car park 5A is on the left, before you are opposite the Jaguar garage.

From the South come up Station Hill Road. At the mini-roundabout, turn right and proceed down the high street, past the shops. Just after the Jaguar garage of the left, car park 5A is on the right hand side.

There are three firework ticket entrances. All are a short walk from the car park.

  • The East Turnstiles (next to Ticket Office East) is available for ticket holders only.
  • Ticket Office East (opposite the police station) will be selling tickets on the the night.
  • Course Road entrance (where the east end of the race course buildings meet Course Road) will be both selling tickets and accepting advanced purchased tickets.
    This entrance is accessed either from Course Road or through the white gates near the roundabout where the High Street and Station Hill meet.

Use the links below to see a map of where the car parks and entrances are this year.

  • Car Park 5A
  • East Turnstiles Entrance (Ticket holders only)
  • Ticket Office East Entrance (Ticket sales)
  • Course Road Entrance (Ticket sales & Advance tickets)

On Foot

The event is in Ascot Racecourse Grandstand.

From Ascot High Street use Racecourse entrance to the East of the main buildings, through white gates opposite roundabout junction between Station Hill and the High Street.

If coming from South Ascot, head towards Ascot High Street. Upon reaching a roundabout at the top of the hill turn left onto the High Street. Cross over road next to car park 3 and continue up the road for approximately 50 metres. Use main Racecourse entrance.

From Ascot railway station follow station path (next to car parks 3 & 4), continue straight up to the High Street, cross road and use the main entrance. Note the station path will be lit for the duration of the event and should be used in preference to the foot path up Station Hill road.
